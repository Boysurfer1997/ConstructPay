1. Overview

ConstructPay is a private, internal-use desktop application for construction payment management, lien waiver tracking, and accounts-payable processing. It integrates with Intuit QuickBooks Online (“QBO”) to synchronize vendor, account, and billing data. ConstructPay is not offered for sale or distribution to the general public.

3. QuickBooks Online Integration

ConstructPay connects to your QuickBooks Online company via Intuit’s OAuth 2.0 authorization flow. By authorizing the connection, you grant ConstructPay permission to read and write the following data within your QBO company: vendors, chart of accounts (GL accounts), projects, and bills (accounts payable). ConstructPay does not access payroll, banking, tax, or employee data. You may revoke this access at any time from the QBO Settings page within ConstructPay or from your Intuit account’s connected-apps settings.

4. Data Storage and Security

All application data is stored locally on the device where ConstructPay is installed. QBO OAuth tokens are stored in the browser’s local storage, scoped per company. No data is transmitted to third-party servers other than Intuit’s QBO API endpoints for the purposes described in Section 3. The Licensor does not collect analytics, telemetry, or usage data.
