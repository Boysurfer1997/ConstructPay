Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 16, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ConstructPay handles information when you use the application. ConstructPay is a private, internal-use desktop tool for construction payment management. It is not a public service and is not offered to the general public.

2. Information We Access

When you connect ConstructPay to your QuickBooks Online company, the application accesses the following QBO data: vendor names and contact information, chart of accounts (GL account names and numbers), QBO projects, and bill/invoice data (amounts, dates, document numbers, line items). ConstructPay does not access payroll records, bank account credentials, tax filings, employee personal information, or customer payment card data.

3. How We Use Your Information

Data accessed from QBO is used solely to power ConstructPay’s core features: syncing AP vendors for invoice processing, mapping GL accounts for expense coding, associating QBO projects with bills, and pushing approved invoices to QBO as bills. No data is used for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond the application’s stated functionality.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the device where ConstructPay is installed. This includes QBO sync data (vendors, GL accounts, projects), invoices and lien waivers managed within the app, and OAuth 2.0 tokens (stored in the browser’s local storage, scoped per company). No data is stored on external servers, cloud databases, or third-party platforms. The only network communication is directly between your device and Intuit’s QBO API.

5. Data Sharing

ConstructPay does not sell, rent, share, or disclose your data to any third parties. Data is transmitted only to Intuit’s QuickBooks Online API as required to perform the synchronization and bill-creation functions you initiate.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Data persists on your local device for as long as you use ConstructPay. You can disconnect from QBO at any time via the QBO Settings page, which removes stored OAuth tokens. Uninstalling ConstructPay removes all locally stored application data. No residual data is retained on any external server.

7. Cookies and Tracking

ConstructPay does not use cookies, analytics tools, tracking pixels, or any form of usage telemetry. No behavioral or usage data is collected.

8. Children’s Privacy

ConstructPay is a business tool and is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your information at all times. You may view, modify, or delete any data by using the application’s interface or by removing the application from your device.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of ConstructPay after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
